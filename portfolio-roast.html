Roast & Toast Website Development

Crafting a digital presence for a boutique coffee shop in Australia

Project Overview

This project involved building a modern, responsive website for Roast & Toast — a cozy coffee shop in Australia. The goal was to provide a warm digital experience that reflected the brand’s rustic charm and high-quality offerings.

Key Features:
  • Homepage with strong hero call-to-action
  • Menus section showcasing coffee and food offerings
  • About & Story page to highlight the café’s mission
  • Contact form and reservation system
